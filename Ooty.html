Botanical garden

  • Located on the slopes of the Doddabetta Peak, it spans over 55 acres.
  • The garden houses a diverse collection of flora, including rare species of trees, shrubs, and plants.
  • Key attractions include a fossil tree trunk believed to be over 20 million years old, an Italian-style garden, and a Toda tribal hut display.
  • Visitors can explore an Italian-style garden and view a Toda tribal hut display, offering cultural insights into the indigenous communities of the region.

Pykara Lake and Falls

  • Located about 20 kilometers from Ooty, Pykara Lake is a scenic reservoir.
  • The lake offers boating facilities amidst serene surroundings. Pykara Falls, located nearby, cascade down in multiple stages amidst dense forests.
  • Visitors can enjoy boat rides on the lake and trekking trails to view the picturesque waterfalls, making it a popular spot for nature lovers and photographers.

Ooty Lake

  • Situated centrally in Ooty, it is an artificial lake surrounded by eucalyptus trees and a lush landscape.
  • Visitors can enjoy boating, paddle boating, and horse riding activities around the lake.
  • It offers a serene environment for relaxation, photography, and enjoying the natural ambiance of Ooty.
  • Horse riding is also available around the lake, providing a unique way to explore the surrounding natural beauty.

Nilagiri mountain Railway

  • Built in the early 20th century, the Nilgiri Mountain Railway is a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
  • The railway covers a distance of 46 kilometers from Mettupalayam to Ooty, ascending through tunnels, bridges, and curves amidst stunning landscapes.
  • Known for its scenic beauty and old-world charm, the toy train journey offers panoramic views of tea estates, forests, and waterfalls.
